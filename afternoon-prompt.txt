Afternoon prompt:

Begin building a website from scratch for a topic of your choice. This should be a single page website (one html file) and should include these items:

1. A navbar affixed to the top of the screen which follows the users as they scroll down the page. Use position:sticky to achieve this. The navbar should use a flexbox for its various links. The links may be purely decorative.

2. A hero image at the top of the screen that contains your h1 title. It's recommended you use a background-image property to achieve this.

3. A footer at the bottom of the screen.

4. Create a column at the center of the screen for your main content. You can achieve this with grid or with margin auto.

5. Add sections of content. You may use lorem ipsum content if you don't want to write your own.

6. Create a section with a grid. You may do this to create a calendar, a picture grid similar to what we've done in class, a map, or anything else you can think up. The ideal here is that you're creating a grid somewhere on the page.

7. Create one media query to handle an issue you find on small screens. A good breakpoint could be max-width: 700px.

8. OPTIONAL STRETCH BONUS: Use bootstrap somewhere!
