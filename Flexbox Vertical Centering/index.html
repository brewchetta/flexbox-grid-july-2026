Our Story

At Peak Gear we make stuff for mountain climbers. You have trust your gear with your life, so we have dedicated our lives to making amazing quality, trustworthy products that we want to use ourselves. When we’re not working, we go climbing with our own stuff. That’s why we go into this business, because we wanted better gear than we could find. So we just had to make it ourselves!

We have tons of different types of gear: climbing shoes, ropes, clothing, ice axes, bags, tents, hand warmers, coats, and more. If you can’t find something you need for your climb contact us and maybe we’ll just have to start making it!
